Sexual Violence Center (SVC) Application

Prime Digital Academy-Group Project

Technologies used: JavaScript, PostgresSQL, Express.js, AngularJS, AngularJS Material, Node.js, Moment.js, Heroku, GitHub

Go To Repo
  • The SVC is a rape crisis center supporting victims of sexual violence in the Twin Cities
  • Previously, the SVC case management tracking process was paper, labor, and time intensive, and it was unable to accurately track metrics for reporting
  • SVC application provides intuitive and efficient tools for SVC employees and volunteers to organize their sexual violence advocacy process, so they can better serve the community
  • SVC administrators are able to log into the SVC app to easily create, track, and update cases as they progress
  • As cases are added, the application automatically collects and displays important data for reporting to assist in securing financial support for the future
